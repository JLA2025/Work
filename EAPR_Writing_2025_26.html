IELTS 8.5-9.0
85-100%
a) Demonstrates original thinking and innovative approaches to sustainable living topic with sophisticated disciplinary connection that goes beyond conventional frameworks
b) Shows original insights and innovative critical thinking. Consistently challenges assumptions with sophisticated questioning that reveals new perspectives and understanding
a) Shows innovative approaches to source identification and evaluation, going beyond conventional academic sources to demonstrate sophisticated research independence
b) Demonstrates original synthesis that creates new insights by connecting sources in innovative ways. Shows exceptional ability to develop independent arguments from source material
a) Shows innovative organisational approaches that enhance argument development. Uses sophisticated structural patterns that go beyond conventional academic formats
b) Demonstrates innovative paragraph development with sophisticated topic sentences that create original connections and insights throughout
a) Shows sophisticated command of academic conventions with innovative use of register and tone that enhances argument effectiveness
b) Demonstrates innovative integration of sources that goes beyond conventional citation to create seamless argument development with exceptional accuracy
IELTS 7.5-8.0
70-84%
a) Shows strong analytical skills within conventional frameworks. Demonstrates comprehensive understanding of sustainable living topic with clear disciplinary connection
b) Demonstrates strong analytical skills with effective critical and questioning approach. Challenges assumptions and evaluates information with clear insight using established analytical methods
a) Demonstrates strong analytical skills in source identification and evaluation within conventional academic research frameworks
b) Shows strong synthesis and application of sources using established academic methods. Develops well-supported arguments through effective source integration
a) Uses strong organisational skills within conventional academic structures. Produces well-organised, coherent text with effective use of organisational patterns
b) Demonstrates strong paragraph development with effective topic sentences that introduce themes clearly using conventional academic approaches
a) Demonstrates strong command of academic conventions with consistent register and tone throughout using established academic practices
b) Shows strong citation and referencing skills with clear quotes/paraphrases/summaries. References and citations generally accurate with only occasional minor errors
IELTS 6.5-7.0
60-69%
a) Very good understanding of sustainable living topic with developing disciplinary connection. Makes comprehensive assessment of issues with evidence of independence
b) Shows developing critical and questioning approach to information with emerging analytical thinking. Demonstrates developing ability to challenge assumptions, though analysis may be superficial at times
a) Very good strategies for source identification and critical evaluation with evidence of originality and independence in research approach
b) Very good synthesis and application. Inclusion of mostly relevant sources with evidence of ability to evaluate and synthesise information systematically
a) Very good organisation with information and ideas arranged coherently. Clear overall progression using variety of cohesive devices effectively
b) Very good topic sentences with clear paragraph focus and effective development throughout most of the essay
a) Academic register and tone generally realised; uses academic structures and vocabulary appropriately, though may be occasional inappropriacy
b) Very good citation and referencing. Uses paraphrase/summary and direct quotation with only minor errors. Citations included but may lack accuracy/clarity
IELTS 5.5-6.0
40-59%
a) Good understanding of sustainable living topic with basic disciplinary connection. Demonstrates understanding of key concepts with clear arguments and basic evaluation
b) Limited critical and questioning approach evident with mainly descriptive rather than analytical approach. Shows little ability to challenge assumptions
a) Good strategies for source identification and evaluation with clear arguments and basic source evaluation attempts
b) Good synthesis and application. Fair range of source material consulted with attempts to evaluate information and develop clear arguments
a) Good organisation. Text generally well-organised and coherent, using linking words and cohesive devices, although there may be some gaps
b) Good use of topic sentences, though may be inconsistent. Generally effective paragraph development with some weak areas
a) Academic register and tone sometimes realised; attempts to use academic structures and vocabulary, but may be some inaccuracies or inappropriate language
b) Good citation and referencing attempts. Paraphrases/summarises relevant content, though with errors or instances of lifting. Referencing attempted but with omissions
IELTS 4.5-5.0
30-39%
a) Adequate understanding of sustainable living topic with minimal disciplinary connection. Demonstrates basic knowledge with adequate attempt to relate to research question
b) No evidence of critical or questioning approach evident. Accepts information without evaluation or challenge. No evidence of analytical thinking
a) Adequate strategies for source identification with minimal evaluation. Demonstrates basic knowledge and attempt to relate to the question
b) Adequate synthesis and application. Sources may be limited or of limited reliability. Very little analysis attempted. Develops basic arguments
a) Adequate organisation. Presents information with some organisation but may lack overall progression. Cohesive devices may be inadequate or over-used
b) Adequate topic sentences but may be unclear or absent. Basic paragraph development with limited effectiveness
a) Limited awareness of appropriate academic register and tone; repeated use of inappropriate language that affects academic credibility
b) Adequate citation attempts. Some attempt at paraphrase/summary but largely unsuccessful with weak paraphrasing and significant lifting. References missing or with significant errors
IELTS 3.5-4.0
10-29%
a) Limited understanding of sustainable living topic with no clear disciplinary connection. Demonstrates very little understanding of main concepts
b) No critical or questioning approach evident. Unable to evaluate or challenge information. No understanding of need to question assumptions
a) Limited strategies for source identification with no evaluation. Very little understanding of main concepts relevant to the research question
b) Limited synthesis and application. No or very limited sources used with little or no analysis. Ideas difficult to identify and may be irrelevant
a) Limited organisation. Information not arranged coherently with no clear progression. Uses some basic cohesive devices but may be inaccurate or repetitive
b) Limited evidence of effective topic sentences. Poor paragraph development with unclear structure
a) Very limited or no awareness of appropriate academic register and tone. Lacks understanding of academic writing conventions
b) Limited citation and referencing. Lacks in all aspects of academic style and conventions - unacceptable as piece of academic writing
IELTS under 3.0
under 10%
a) Very limited understanding of sustainable living topic. Not relevant to the research question with no evidence of academic content
b) No evidence of critical thinking or questioning approach. Unable to engage with information in any analytical way
a) Very limited strategies for source identification. Not relevant to the title with no evidence of academic content or source evaluation
b) Very limited synthesis and application. No analysis or evaluation attempted. Response barely related to task with no clear position
a) Very limited organisation. Fails to organise information logically. Uses only most important connecting words ("first", "then", "after", "later")
b) Very limited or no evidence of topic sentences. No effective paragraph structure evident
a) Lacks in all aspects of academic style and conventions - unacceptable as piece of academic writing
b) Very limited citation and referencing. Lacks in all aspects of academic style and conventions - unacceptable as piece of academic writing
U No attempt made, or unable to assess criteria due to the amount of lifting from the source texts
